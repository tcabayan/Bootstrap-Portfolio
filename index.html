About Me

Hi Everyone!

So my website is finally here, or at least in part! This site is a work in progress, but in the meantime I wanted to tell you a bit about myself as well as about my work!

This website and my portfolio are a testament to all that I learned in my journey to learn basic computer coding as well as my blood, sweat, and tears (but I didn't create a separate portfolio for that!).
